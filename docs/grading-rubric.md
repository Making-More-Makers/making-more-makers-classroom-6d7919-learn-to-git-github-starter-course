## ğŸ† Final Project (50 points total)

**Due**: End of Day 5  
**Components**: Code repository, documentation, demo video, GitHub Pages, Day 6 presentation

### 1. Functionality (20 points)

| Criteria | Excellent (18-20) | Good (14-17) | Satisfactory (10-13) | Needs Improvement (0-9) |
|----------|------------------|--------------|-------------------|------------------------|
| **Core Features** (8 pts) | All planned features fully implemented and working flawlessly | Most features working well | Core features working with issues | Many features missing or broken |

| **Code Quality** (6 pts) | Clean, modular, well-commented code; follows best practices | Good code structure with adequate comments | Working code but poorly organized | Poor code quality or doesn't work |

### 2. Documentation (10 points)

| Criteria | Excellent (9-10) | Good (7-8) | Satisfactory (5-6) | Needs Improvement (0-4) |
|----------|-----------------|------------|-------------------|------------------------|

| **Media** (2 pts) | High-quality photos, diagrams, and demo video | Good media included | Some media | No or poor quality media |

### 3. Presentation (10 points)

**Format**: 10-minute presentation + 5-minute Q&A on Day 6

| Criteria | Excellent (9-10) | Good (7-8) | Satisfactory (5-6) | Needs Improvement (0-4) |

| **Live Demo** (3 pts) | Smooth, impressive live demonstration | Demo works with minor issues | Demo partially works | Demo fails or not attempted |
| **Clarity & Delivery** (2 pts) | Confident, clear, engaging presentation | Clear presentation | Adequate presentation | Unclear or unprepared |
| **Time Management** (1 pt) | Perfect timing (9-10 minutes) | Close to time limit | Too short or too long | Significantly off time |

### 4. Innovation & Creativity (10 points)

| Criteria | Excellent (9-10) | Good (7-8) | Satisfactory (5-6) | Needs Improvement (0-4) |
|----------|-----------------|------------|-------------------|------------------------|

| **Technical Complexity** (3 pts) | Advanced techniques; goes beyond course basics | Good technical challenge | Adequate technical level | Too simple |
| **Problem Solving** (3 pts) | Creative solutions to difficult problems; excellent troubleshooting | Good problem solving | Basic problem solving | Minimal problem solving |

## ğŸ“Œ Special Considerations

### Bonus Points Opportunities (up to +5%)

- **Outstanding Documentation**: +2 points for exceptionally clear and comprehensive documentation
- **Community Contribution**: +2 points for helping multiple classmates with significant issues
- **Innovation Award**: +3 points for most innovative project (selected by instructors)
- **Technical Excellence**: +2 points for implementing advanced features beyond requirements

### Late Submission Policy

- **Penalty**: 10% deduction per day late
- **Maximum**: 3 days late accepted (after 3 days = 0 points)
- **Extensions**: Granted only for documented emergencies

### Resubmission Policy

- **Assignments 1-3**: One resubmission allowed within 2 days for up to 80% of lost points
- **Final Project**: No resubmissions after Day 5 deadline
